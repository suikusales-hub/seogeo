450% Organic Traffic Growth in 8 Months

How strategic SEO transformed a manufacturing company's online presence

Client Overview

  • Industry: Industrial Manufacturing
  • Company Size: 200+ employees
  • Target Market: Global B2B
  • Website: WordPress + WooCommerce

The Challenge

A leading industrial equipment manufacturer was struggling with poor online visibility despite having high-quality products. Their website received less than 500 organic visitors per month, and most of their leads came from expensive trade shows and cold calling.

Key Issues Identified:

  • Technical SEO problems affecting crawlability
  • Poor keyword targeting and content strategy
  • Weak backlink profile and domain authority
  • Non-optimized product pages and descriptions
  • Lack of international SEO implementation

Our SEO Strategy

1

Technical Foundation (Months 1-2)

  • Comprehensive technical audit and fixes
  • Site architecture optimization
  • Core Web Vitals improvement
  • Mobile optimization and responsiveness
2

Content & Keywords (Months 3-4)

  • Comprehensive keyword research and mapping
  • Product page optimization
  • Technical blog content creation
  • Landing page development for key products
3

Authority Building (Months 5-8)

  • Strategic link building campaign
  • Industry partnership development
  • Trade publication content marketing
  • International market expansion

Measurable Results

450% Organic Traffic Increase

From 500 to 2,750 monthly organic visitors

127 Keywords Ranking Page 1

85% improvement in keyword rankings

320% Lead Generation Increase

From 15 to 63 monthly qualified leads

280% Revenue Attribution

Organic traffic contributing $1.2M annually

Key Learnings & Takeaways

Technical SEO First: Fixing technical issues provided the foundation for all other improvements.

Industry-Specific Content: Technical, in-depth content significantly outperformed generic marketing content.

B2B Link Building: Industry partnerships and trade publications were more effective than generic link building.
